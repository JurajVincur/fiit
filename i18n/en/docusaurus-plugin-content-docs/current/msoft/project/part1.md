# Project Intent

**Format:** PDF  
**Submission:** before the 3rd exercise  

## Assignment
- Specify the general theme. The general theme for this semester is **"Smart Home Systems"**.  
- The project intent is understood as a textual description of the system you will be modeling, with a length of **250 – 400 words**.  
- The intent must clearly state what **functionalities** the system will provide and **who** will interact with it (its intended users).  
- The project must have a **name**.  
- The intent must be **approved during the exercises**; modifications may be required according to the instructor’s feedback.  

## Evaluation (max. 5 points)
- Clear and well-structured intent – **5 points**
